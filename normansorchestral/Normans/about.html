About Norman's Orchestral Center

Norman’s Orchestral Center opened in 2015 and is a locally owned business in Rochester that promotes classical music. Norman Hartley opened the store with the intention to create a welcoming space where customers could feel at home that offers a variety of services in which classical music can be appreciated. Here at Norman’s Orchestral Center, we offer instrument repairs, lessons, and sell vintage instruments. If you are interested in selling a used specialty or vintage instrument, please contact us. We also hold musical events that showcase our students and local musicians.
